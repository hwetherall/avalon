Business idea:

Creating a software enabled business that provides a wine inventory management system with a virtual sommelier front end to private clubs (country clubs, social clubs, etc) that have fine dining (assumed to have large wine collections). The system addresses the following pain points for private country club (and other equivalent clubs):

Updated digital wine list - real time

Pairing of food menu to wine lists

Optimal drinking windows

Dynamic pricing to incentivize consumption and inventory turnover efficiency

Actual link between inventory availability and the wine lists

Extending Master Sommelier status to be available to customers and clients to ALL servers in ALL shifts

Many times it is not one single cellar but multiple locations

Mark to market of the current inventory. Most country clubs hold inventory at COST and not MARKET VALUE. This could be hundreds of thousands of dollars of unlocked value.


The ultimate goal is to increase client experience for these clubs around wine consumption (optimal pairing, updated lists with real time availability, price transparency, best pick and recommendation within price windows), and ultimately provide a master sommelier experience to all clients at all times. Also pre-selecting and decanting wines pre-arrival.


Additional revenue opportunities could come from member consignment of their own collections, and possibly to track individual preferences.


THE CURRENT PLATFORM CAPABILITIES:

The platform exists (www.cellareye.com) and has been operational for some time. The primary features that are available today that can be leveraged to support the vision above include:

Tracking the location of wine bottles using visual references to individual bottles

An NFC tag labeled region of a cellar tracks the location of 12 bottles at a time

A full stack application that manages the inventory

Access to real time pricing and reviews of 500,000 wines across the world

A virtual sommelier app with wine pairing, drinking windows, and preference based recommendations

Temperature, humidity, and access control monitoring service through third party IOT sensors for optimal cellar control and management

Audit and report generation - automatically - across one or more cellars

Multi-cellar management
